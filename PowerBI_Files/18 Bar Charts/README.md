# 📊 18 Ways to Visualize Bar Charts in Power BI

**Date:** 2026-04-20  
**Version:** 1.0.0  

---

## 📝 Description

A comprehensive collection demonstrating **18 different ways to visualize bar charts** natively in Power BI.  
Inspired by Andy Kriebel's original Tableau visuals and Rosa Mariana do León-Escribano's design, this Power BI adaptation by Nicholas Lea-Trengrouse showcases the incredible flexibility of standard visuals.

It includes popular variations such as:  
- **Standard & Stacked:** Basic, Outline, Rounded, Stacked, and Divergent  
- **Comparisons:** Butterfly, Bar in Bar, Side by Side, and Bullet Graphs  
- **Progress & Tracking:** Progress Bars (Standard, Thin, Area) and Lollipop  
- **Advanced/Creative:** Candlestick, Line End, and Jittered Bar Charts  

This report serves as a visual dictionary and technical template for pushing the boundaries of standard Power BI charts.

## 🧭 How to Use

1. Download the `.pbix` file.  
2. Open it in Power BI Desktop.  
3. Review the layout, formatting panes, and measures for each specific chart variation to see how the effects are achieved.  
4. Reuse or adapt the specific chart patterns in your own reports.
